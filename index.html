From Brushes to Binary

Our journey into the fusion of art and technology

My Journey into Tech-Art

Started with traditional sketching in high school, got obsessed with Processing during university, and now I spend way too much time making pixels dance. Every piece is a experiment in pushing what code can create.

The Process (It's Messy)

I usually start with a wild idea at 2AM, spend hours debugging why my particles won't move right, then accidentally create something completely different that looks way cooler than what I planned.

Why This Matters

Art doesn't have to be painted on canvas or carved from stone. Code is just another medium - one that lets you create things that breathe, react, and evolve. That's the future I'm excited about.
